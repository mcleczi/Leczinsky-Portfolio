About Me

I am a Computer Science and Business Intelegence double major at Clarkson University.

Academics

I am a CS and Business Intelegence double major at Clarkson University. I'm also minoring in both Math and Project Managment. Currently, I hold a 3.701 GPA and am in my Junior Year. I have been on the Dean's list every semester at Clarkson and Presidents list twice.

I have taken courses such as: Algorithms and Data Structures I/II, Advanced Programming Concepts in Java, Operation Systems Software Design and Development, Front-End Development, Database Design and Development and Computer and Network Security, to name a few.

Career Goals

To use my relevant technical knowledge and coursework to obtain a career in software engineering or a related field. I hope to have upward mobility and room to move in my career. Computer science and coding is certainly my passion and I hope to make it a career as soon as I graduate.

Currently, I am working at Moog Inc. in Elma NY, as a Co-op working on internal web applications. I hope to use this experience to grow and develop my skillset as a software engineer and web developer.
